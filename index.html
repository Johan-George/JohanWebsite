Hi I'm Johan!

Hello, I’m Johan and I’m an South-Indian Canadian musician and student at Fanshawe College starting the Music Industry Arts program in Fall 2022.

From 2019 - present I have been writing, producing, arranging and transcribing music.

I’ve been playing the piano since I was 10 years old until I discovered a deeper passion for Performance and Music Theory in highschool.

After entering an Air Cadet marching band as a Glockenspiel Player, I eventually transitioned to the Snare Drum which I have excelled in, performing in drumlines, and leading parades as my band’s lead snare drummer.

In my high school photography class I discovered Garageband and Bandlab and learned to make music with preset loops.

This lead me on the path to eventually pursuing a career in music production.
